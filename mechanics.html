Headliner

Headliners in TFT are the revamped version of Chosen units, offering a fresh twist to the gameplay. Purchased at 2-stars from the shop, they provide an extra +1 towards a specific trait and boast unique Headliner bonuses. Unlike Chosen, every shop now features a Headliner, eliminating the randomness of encountering them. Plus, even if you already have one, new Headliners appear every fourth shop, allowing for strategic pivots and upgrades throughout the game. Remember, to acquire a new Headliner, you'll need to sell your current one. This updated mechanic adds depth and strategic complexity to TFT, fostering memorable gameplay experiences and "that one game I had..." stories.

Updated Portals

In Remix Rumble, Portals play a supporting role to the main focus on Headliners, offering game-altering effects with a more straightforward design and nomenclature. Unlike their predecessors in Runeterra Reforged, which could be complex and game-defining, the Portals in Remix Rumble have a lower ceiling of impact. While still capable of significantly altering gameplay dynamics, they're designed to be easier to understand and remember. This shift allows for greater flexibility to introduce and adjust Portals throughout the set or future iterations, ensuring a balanced and engaging experience for players.

New Augments

In Remix Rumble, Augments continue to play a crucial role in shaping the distinctiveness of each game. With over 40 new Augments and several returning from previous sets, players are provided with a diverse array of tools to explore and experiment with. The focus is on striking a balance between introducing exciting and deep Augments while avoiding those that overly dictate specific compositions or become too complex for casual players to enjoy. Whether you're drawn to the unconventional or prefer straightforward stat boosts, there's something for everyone among the Augments. Take, for instance, the wildly innovative "Blank Slate," just one example of the myriad possibilities awaiting players in Remix Rumble.
